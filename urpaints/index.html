About URAI PAINTS

U.R. CHEMICAL Co., Ltd. is the manufacturer and distributor of decorative paints, industrial paints, spray paints, wood coatings, and chemicals for construction and repairs. Over decades, the company has been operating with the principles of good governance including social responsibility, aiming to produce high-quality products at reasonable price in response to customer demand.
